About Us

RY Automation Resources is a locally established automation enterprise that provides specialized services in PLC programming, SCADA/HMI integration, basic control panel wiring and troubleshooting, as well as VFD installation and programming. In addition to services, we also supply a range of quality automation products such as proximity sensors, photoelectric sensors, PLC Devices, HMI, and VFD to meet various industrial needs.

As a growing automation service and solution provider, we are committed to supporting industries with reliable, innovative, and cost-effective solutions. We take pride in offering hands-on, personalized service — whether it's delivering a product, troubleshooting an issue, or developing a full automation system.
