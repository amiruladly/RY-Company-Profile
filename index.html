About Us

Founded in 2025, RY Automation Resources is an emerging automation solutions provider led by a passionate team of engineering students dedicated to shaping the future of industrial automation in Malaysia. While still pursuing our academic journey, we are actively building real-world expertise by delivering smart, reliable, and cost-effective automation, control, and monitoring systems for small to medium enterprises and academic institutions.

Our growing capabilities include custom automation design, smart factory integration, and intelligent control systems using Programmable Logic Controllers (PLC), SCADA, and Internet of Things (IoT) technologies.
